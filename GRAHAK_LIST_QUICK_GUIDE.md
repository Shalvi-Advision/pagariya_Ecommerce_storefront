## рҹҡҖ What is Grahak List?

**Grahak List** (Customer List) is your personal monthly grocery manager! Create lists, add items, track purchases, and reorder with just one click.

### 2. **Smart Suggestions** рҹӨ–

### 3. **Easy Management** вң…
- Add/remove items
